Travel Blog


This blog is an extension of my STEM Grade 12 project, a vlog documenting our travel journey from Gingoog to Cagayan De Oro. Packing commenced before our departure, with numerous pit stops along the way to allow our driver, my father, to rest. Nervous excitement gripped me as my sister urged me to begin recording the video. Although I had experience with casual video recording on my cellphone, creating a vlog proved to be a daunting task. Crafting a captivating vlog necessitates meticulous effort to compile numerous recordings—often totaling between 20 to 30—into a coherent narrative. As I navigated through the editing process, I realized the intricacies involved in weaving together disparate moments into a cohesive story. Each clip, whether capturing an epic adventure or a lighthearted moment, required careful consideration for pacing and narrative flow. Despite the challenges, witnessing the final product—a montage of our journey's highlights—filled me with a sense of pride and accomplishment.


During our travels, amidst laughter and jokes, I attempted a humorous remark about the charm of watching dramas, which unexpectedly sparked amusement from my parents. However, my intended phrase of 'how dare they' was inadvertently replaced with 'who dare they,' resulting in a moment of light embarrassment. As we journeyed onwards, passing through Balingasag, my attention was drawn to the familiar sight of a gas station adjacent to a bank. Feeling the need for a pit stop after indulging in a snack of boiled eggs, I suggested a brief bathroom break. Before long, our car rolled into Cagayan De Oro, catching me off guard amidst our banter and jokes. Our plans for the day included grabbing breakfast at Seafood Haven, followed by a visit to the furniture store nestled behind Centrio Mall. My sister's goal for a new chair led us to stumble upon a 'Gaming Chair,' sparking a lively debate on its merits. As we embarked on a journey to Xavier University, where my sister was eager to share her experiences, I found myself contemplating the allure of a gaming chair. Our excursion continued at Centrio Mall, where we explored various electronics stores, scrutinizing prices for headphones and other accessories for my Playstation."

After our trip in Centrio, We started our journey with prayers at Divine Mercy, lighting candles for our intentions. Then, we made our way to S & R mall for groceries and a satisfying lunch. Resting for a night at a hotel in Estrella, we recharged for the next day's adventures. Gaisano beckoned us for more shopping, where we began with a hearty breakfast at Chowking. Exploring the mall, we browsed for new outfits and necessities, taking our time on each floor. The food court provided a delightful break, where we enjoyed delicious meals and refreshing drinks. As the day progressed, we decided on an early dinner at Talisyan Seafood Restaurant, discovering a new park nearby with exciting entertainment options. Capturing memories through photos, we savored the moments before heading home. Our dog welcomed us with wagging tails, signaling the end of our fulfilling travels through Cagayan. The Video will show its proof.
