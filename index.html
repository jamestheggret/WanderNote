About Us


In the bustling metropolis of Sumaru City, The Protagonist and his friends, embark on a journey of self-discovery and redemption within the enigmatic realm of the Shadow Symphony created by Maestro. As they navigate treacherous dungeons and engage in strategic battles against formidable adversaries, each character confronts their inner demons and forges deep bonds with their personas. Within the digital pages of WanderNote, readers are immersed in a tale of resilience, camaraderie, and the enduring power of the human spirit, as He and his friends strive to illuminate the shadows threatening to engulf their world and forge a path towards salvation.


Despite the trials and tribulations that beset their world, The Protagonist and his friends remain steadfast in their resolve to craft enduring memories. As they stand on the brink of parting ways, their hearts heavy with the weight of impending separation, they are fueled by a shared determination to seize the present moment. Each click of the camera, each shared laugh, becomes a testament to their unyielding spirit and unwavering friendship. For them, the pursuit of memories transcends the confines of time and space, serving as a beacon of hope in the face of uncertainty. As they embark on their individual paths, they carry with them the indelible imprint of their shared adventures, a testament to the enduring power of camaraderie and the bonds forged amidst life's trials.
